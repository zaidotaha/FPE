About me!

Hey there! I am a software engineer in the making based in Amman, Jordan. I am currently a Computer Science student at the University of Jordan and I'm graduating this fall semester! I look forward to joining the exciting and growing tech space in Jordan. However, rather than boring you with my 5 year plan for the future let me tell you more about myself.

I was born in Amman, but I moved to Sharjah at the age of 6 where I grew up. I have switched schools 5 times, so change is something I am completely comfortable with. After graduating highschool, I moved back to Jordan for university. Unfortunately, Computer Science wasn't my first major, Medicine was. After 3 gruelling years pursuing a degree I was not passionate about I decided to switch majors and I never looked back since!

Now, in my last semester here at UJ, I am honing my tech skills and developing them further. I am training at Orange Coding School, doing side projects and expanding my toolset by learning new technologies. The next few years will be an exciting endevour for me, and I can't wait!
